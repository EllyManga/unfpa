Scope of Work

How We Drive Menstrual Health Reform

Our approach is multi-layered, combining digital platforms, community engagement, and policy advocacy to create lasting change.

  • 01

    Site Architecture & Information Design

    We develop clear wireframes and site structures that make health information accessible to all Kenyan audiences.

  • 02

    UI/UX Aligned to Advocacy Goals

    Responsive, intuitive design that serves girls, educators, policymakers, and healthcare workers equally.

  • 03

    Content Integration & Knowledge Sharing

    Reports, visuals, and resources are integrated into a searchable, shareable content hub.

  • 04

    Testing, Optimisation & Handover

    Thorough testing and full training handover to ensure the NAYA team can sustain the platform independently.
